Shuai Wang

Shuai Wang is now a researcher working with Huawei Noah's Ark Lab. His current research interests include recommender systems and responsible AI. He has published several papers on conferences in AI, NLP and data mining fields. He has been involved in several commercial projects and promoted the business success with AI algorithms
