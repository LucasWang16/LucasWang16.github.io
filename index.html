Shuai Wang

Shuai Wang is now a researcher working with Huawei Noah's Ark Lab. His current research interests include Large Language Models (especally in supervised finetune), information retrieval with LLM and bio-medical informatics. He has published several papers on conferences in AI, data mining and interdisciplinary fields. He has been involved in several commercial projects and promoted the business success with AI algorithms.
